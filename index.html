Life of a Stoic

September 27, 2022 by Callay Jarrett

The great Philosophers left us with a wealth of knowledge to help guide us along the way. Many of these knowledge sets came in the form of schools - my favourite being Stoicism. As I read the teachings of Epictetus and Marcus Aurelius, I am more energized to live life and continue on my day. Though Stoicism doesn't guarantee that life will be perfect, it sure does a wonderful job of letting me realize that I can only control so much and no more. This mindset brings me so much solace.

Stoicism sees wisdom as the highest virtue. It strives to achieve good character, and an understanding of preferred indifferences. Life will never be perfect, but with a little philosophy in our lives it becomes more bearable. I don't know where life will take me, but I want to always have the knowledge of those before me in my arsenal.

Film-Making

October 2, 2022 by Callay Jarrett

I am more and more excited for a life after school every time I think about doing film. It is one of the few things in life that I am excited about. I am looking forward to being on set directing, or writing scripts. I want to be able to tell stories that move people and get people to act in positive ways.

I have been practising with my camera and just having fun making different types of content. Putting my imagination to the test and see how far it takes me. I want when people see these stories, they are inspired to continue watching. I want them to be engaged and just in love with the content that I make. I am looking forward to working with industry professionals and really get my head in the game.
